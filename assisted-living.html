ii.Your own home on Broad Oak Lane

The independence of your own home, with care if you want it.

29 luxury houses on Broad Oak Lane, each with its own front door. Live independently if you prefer — or add a tailored care package, anywhere from one to 24 hours a day, as your needs change.

01What life looks like here

A community, on the lane behind the manor.

Broad Oak Lane runs through the grounds of the manor itself. Each of our 29 houses is a self-contained home, thoughtfully designed for older living.

You keep your own life. Your own kitchen, your own friends, your own front door. Some residents need no day-to-day support at all. Others choose a care package, anywhere from one to 24 hours a day, that grows and shrinks as life changes.

The properties are so thoughtfully designed — the underfloor heating has been absolutely amazing. Cost-effective to run, and constantly keeping Mum at a warm temperature all year round.

03Care that fits you, not the other way round

Three care packages. One home.

Independence comes first. Many residents simply enjoy their own home with the community around them — no care package at all. And when you do want support, it’s never one-size-fits-all.

Level One

Home support

For people who continue to live independently in their own home, who don’t require personal care, but who would like help with certain tasks.

  • Meal preparation
  • Light housework
  • Shopping
  • Companionship
Level Two

Home support & care

Everything in Level One, plus a limited amount of personal care.

  • Help with washing and dressing
  • Help into the bath or shower
  • Continence care
  • Everything from Level One
Level Three

Home support & advanced care

For those whose care needs have grown more substantial, but who wish to remain in the home they love.

  • Full personal care and continence care
  • Mobility, transfers and specialist equipment
  • Liaison with GP, district nurses and specialists
  • Everything from Levels One and Two
