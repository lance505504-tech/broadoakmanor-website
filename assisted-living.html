03Care that fits you

Three care packages. One home.

Level One

Home support

For people who live independently but would like help with certain tasks.

  • Meal preparation
  • Light housework
  • Shopping
  • Companionship
Level Two

Home support & care

Everything in Level One, plus a limited amount of personal care.

  • Help with washing and dressing
  • Help into the bath or shower
  • Continence care
  • Everything from Level One
Level Three

Advanced care

For those whose care needs have grown more substantial, but who wish to remain in the home they love.

  • Full personal and continence care
  • Mobility and transfers
  • GP and specialist liaison
  • Everything from Levels One and Two
