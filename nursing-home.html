i.Care within the manor house

Live in the manor, with a nurse on hand day and night.

23 private bedrooms inside our Queen Anne, Grade II listed manor house. Skilled nursing care, communal rooms warmed by daylight, and the gardens just beyond the front door.

01What it means to live here

A room of your own, in a house full of life.

You have your own private bedroom in the manor, furnished to your taste, with views over the grounds or the courtyard. Outside your door is a household that gathers each day — for breakfast around the long dining table, in the lounge by the fire, in the activities room when the singer comes on a Wednesday.

A registered nurse is on the premises twenty-four hours a day. The medicines, the meals, the appointments, the laundry — all of it is held, quietly, by a team that has done this for more than thirty-five years.

My mother stayed here for palliative care. The staff did everything in their power to make it enjoyable, comfortable and homely.
